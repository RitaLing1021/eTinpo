Care Instructions

Regular maintenance ensures product performance and extends its lifespan.

Washing Instructions

Regular cleaning, drying and reactivating the DWR ensure the performance and lifespan of your equipment. Please be sure to read the product's washing and maintenance instructions carefully to optimize its functionality while experiencing eTINPO.

Pre-wash instructions

Zipper care: before washing, zip up all the zippers, including pockets. For heavily soiled areas, pre-treat with detergent for targeted cleaning, helping protect the garment and ensure effective cleanliness.

Laundry instructions
  1. Avoid fabric softener, bleach, soap, laundry powder, and similar detergents. It is recommended to use a neutral laundry detergent.
  2. Wash only one garment at a time, and avoid mixing with other soiled clothes.
  3. Use a low spin speed in the washing machine.
  4. Rinse twice with clean water to remove the detergent.
After washing

Dryer: use a low to medium temperature for gentle drying, avoiding high-temperature drying.
Drying method: it is recommended to air dry naturally. Avoid dry cleaning or tumble drying.

DWR reactivation

Utilize the dryer in a low-temperature setting for 20 minutes.
Alternatively, employ gentle ironing with a cloth between the iron and the garment at a mild temperature.
